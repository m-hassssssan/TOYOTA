Our Story

DRIVING INNOVATION SINCE 1937

From our humble beginnings in a small Japanese loom works to becoming one of the world's largest automakers, Toyota has always been driven by a simple philosophy: continuous improvement and respect for people.

Today, we're not just building cars—we're shaping the future of mobility. With a commitment to sustainability, safety, and cutting-edge technology, we're creating vehicles that inspire and empower people around the globe.

MISSION & VISION

To attract and attain customers with high-valued products and services and the most satisfying ownership experience in America. We strive to exceed expectations and build lasting relationships with every customer.

Our Journey

HISTORY OF EXCELLENCE

1937

THE BEGINNING

Kiichiro Toyoda establishes Toyota Motor Corporation, spinning off from his father's loom manufacturing business.

1957

ENTERING AMERICA

Toyota establishes its first presence in the United States with Toyota Motor Sales, USA.

1966

COROLLA LAUNCH

The Toyota Corolla is introduced, destined to become the best-selling car of all time.

1997

HYBRID REVOLUTION

The Prius becomes the world's first mass-produced hybrid vehicle, changing the automotive landscape forever.

2009

WORLD'S LARGEST

Toyota becomes the world's largest automaker, a testament to quality and customer trust.

2020

ELECTRIC FUTURE

Launch of the Toyota bZ series, marking our commitment to a fully electrified future.

TODAY

INNOVATION CONTINUES

Leading the industry in hydrogen fuel cell technology, autonomous driving, and sustainable manufacturing.
