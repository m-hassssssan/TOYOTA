Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How do I schedule a test drive?
You can schedule a test drive by filling out our contact form, calling your local dealer, or visiting any Toyota dealership. We'll confirm your appointment and have your preferred vehicle ready.
What financing options are available?
Toyota Financial Services offers a variety of financing and leasing options. Our team can help you find the best plan based on your budget and needs. Apply online or at any dealership.
How often should I service my Toyota?
We recommend service every 5,000 miles or 6 months, whichever comes first. This includes oil changes, tire rotations, and multi-point inspections. Check your owner's manual for specific recommendations.
What is ToyotaCare?
ToyotaCare is a no-cost maintenance plan that comes with every new Toyota for 2 years or 25,000 miles, whichever comes first. It includes factory-scheduled maintenance and 24/7 roadside assistance.
